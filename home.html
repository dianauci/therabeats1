The Problem: Stress Among College Students

College life can be overwhelming, with academic pressure, social demands, and career anxieties contributing to high levels of stress. Unfortunately, chronic stress can negatively impact both mental and physical health, leading to burnout, anxiety, and decreased productivity. Many students struggle to find effective, personalized tools to manage their stress. That's where TheraBeats comes in, offering a solution tailored specifically to help students cope with these challenges in real-time.

How It Works

TheraBeats seamlessly integrates with fitness trackers like the Apple Watch to monitor real-time physiological indicators such as heart rate variability. When the app detects rising stress levels through changes in heart rate variability, it automatically suggests calming music or soundscapes tailored to the user's preferences. These personalized audio tracks are designed to help lower stress and promote relaxation, offering an intuitive way for users to manage their stress levels effectively.

Key Benefits

Unlike traditional stress-tracking apps, TheraBeats takes a proactive approach by providing real-time intervention rather than just tracking symptoms. Key benefits include:

  • Real-time stress management: Automatically detects stress and provides immediate intervention through personalized music therapy.
  • Personalized solutions: Unlike generic playlists, TheraBeats tailors music recommendations based on user preferences and biofeedback.
  • Designed for college students: Addresses stressors unique to students, such as academic pressure, career anxiety, and social balance.
  • Data-driven insights: Tracks stress patterns over time to help users develop healthier coping strategies.

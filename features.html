Listen and Personalize

  • Listen to your custom playlists with songs you enjoy when feeling stressed.
  • Get recommendations for scientifically proven soundscapes to reduce stress.
  • Explore pre-made playlists tailored to common college stressors like academic pressure and career anxiety.

Customizable Listening Experience

  • Play music from your streaming services or choose scientifically proven soundscapes for stress relief.
  • Save your favorite songs and soundscapes for easy access later.
  • Receive more personalized recommendations based on your favorites.

Connect and Share

  • Share songs with friends and send messages directly in TheraBeats.
  • Recommend songs to friends and chat about your experiences.
  • Strengthen connections while discovering new music together.

Real-Time Stress Feedback

  • See how your heart rate and stress levels change while listening to a song.
  • Track how different songs affect your stress in real time.
  • Gain insights to discover which music helps you relax the most.

Track Your Progress

  • View a graph of your stress levels on a weekly or monthly basis.
  • Track key biofeedback metrics like body temperature, heart rate, and skin conductance.
  • Monitor your sleep patterns and see trends over time.
  • Gain valuable insights into your well-being and stress patterns.

Personalize Your Profile

  • Choose how to receive song recommendations: push notification, vibration alert, or both.
  • Set notification frequency: automatic (when stress is detected) or customized (e.g., daily, at night).
  • Connect your preferred music streaming service.
  • Create and manage your playlists directly from your profile.

Real-Time Stress Alerts

  • Get push notifications when TheraBeats detects stress.
  • The notification will ask if you'd like to play calming music.
  • Quickly reduce stress with your favorite songs or recommended soundscapes.
  • Stay in control with real-time support.

Integrating with a Fitness Tracker

  • Get notified with a vibration when stress is detected, and choose to play suggested music.
  • View your current stress level and track your stress patterns.
  • Listen to music while monitoring your current heart rate in real time.
